Style guide

Colors

For the colors on my blog I am going to go with Blue, White and Gold. This could definitely change as I get further in to the project, but for now I am concentrating my site on being fast, accessible and clean

Typography

The Typefaces I am using are both Google fonts. Unoriginally, I am using a Serif typeface for the Headings and a sans-serif font for body text. The typefaces may be subject to change as the design grows, but for now I have focussed on accessibility, so legibility is key.
